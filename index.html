About Us

At Data-Driven insights, we are committed to collecting, analysing, and visualising data to enable meaningful journalism. Our mission is to transform complex datasets into clear, impactful stories that highlight trends and inform solutions. In an age where misinformation often clouds critical issues, we believe legitimate data journalism is key to understanding and addressing challenges effectively. Through rigorous research and innovative visualisations, we empower journalists to deliver narratives that matter.

Our Projects

Dive into our data-driven initiatives that bring clarity and insights to journalism. Each project is a testament to the power of data in uncovering stories that matter

Exploring Industry Comfort with AI Tools

This project investigates how different industries perceive AI tools, highlighting leaders like technology and healthcare and sectors like legal and creative arts showing resistance. It offers a detailed look into trends and challenges shaping AI adoption.

AI Usage Across Writing Tasks

This study focuses on the adoption of AI tools for various writing tasks, revealing that essays and creative writing dominate while social media and reports see limited use. The findings emphasise AI's role in enhancing structured content creation.

Mobile Writing and AI Comfort Levels

This project examines how frequently people write on mobile devices and its impact on AI comfort. Frequent writers show high familiarity with AI, while occasional users display more hesitation, highlighting varying adoption patterns.
